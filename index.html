Where am I From?

I'm a 2nd-generation American Chinese born in New York City, but I grew up in Great Neck, Long Island. My parents are both from Hong Kong. I'm a product of two worlds, one being the traditionally Chinese environment taught by my parents, and the American environment that interacting with my friends and teachers in grade school provided. I cherish this upbringing, as it gives me a truly unique heritage and cultural diversity. While I admit that I may have a bit more of an American outlook than a Chinese outlook by now, I do believe in keeping traditions and roots alive.

Experiences

I went to elementary, middle, and high school in Great Neck, and I loved the feelings of home and safety a suburban town gave. Great Neck was a sort of bubble, where everything inside our town was very self-contained and safe. Growing up and being old enough to explore New York CIty was quite an experience. There was so much around every corner to do! After high school, I attended City College of the City University of New York, and I now work as a financial assistant at Memorial Sloan Kettering Cancer Center. Working here has really expanded my knowledge of the greater world and given me an indepth knowledge of cancer healthcare.

Likes

To me, food is the one thing I can always look forward to. I literally like everything (besides celery) and so the thought of discovering new delicious and exotic foods is one of my reasons of living. I also enjoy reading and watching fantasy, sci-fi, romance novels and movies. Such works can transport you to either a fantastical or unique world apart from ours, and I love exploring what that could feel like. In the same vein, traveling to other parts of the world fulfills this love of exploration as well. Now that I'm in the workforce with an income, I quite enjoy traveling and discovering other cultures and food!
